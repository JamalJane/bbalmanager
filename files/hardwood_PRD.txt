HARDWOOD MANAGER
Product Requirements Document
Version 1.0 | March 2026 | Status: In Design
Stack: React + Vite | Tailwind CSS | Supabase

A web-based basketball franchise management game built with React + Supabase.

================================================================================
1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
================================================================================

1.1 Vision
-----------
Hardwood Manager is a web-based basketball franchise management game with a
clean vintage aesthetic. The player takes on the role of a head GM, building a
franchise from the ground up across multiple seasons. Every decision — who to
draft, how to develop players, who to sign, how to manage the locker room —
compounds over time into a unique franchise story.

The core differentiator is a living franchise narrative. Players are not static
numbers. They have personas, development pathways, signature tendencies,
chemistry with teammates, and careers that arc in response to your decisions.
The game feels like a sports documentary you are directing in real time.

1.2 Target Audience
-------------------
- Sports management game fans (Football Manager, NBA 2K MyGM, Retro Bowl)
- Basketball fans who want strategy over simulation
- Players who enjoy narrative-driven games with meaningful decisions
- Desktop browser users, age 16-35

1.3 Key Differentiators
------------------------
- Player persona system: 8 archetypes, 79 sub-personas, each affecting
  development, chemistry, and story
- Development pathways: 8 pathways per player, persona-pathway compatibility
  matrix drives growth
- Signature system: Every developed player gets a tendency, signature move,
  and quirk
- Franchise narrative: Template-based story beats generated from real game data
- Living league: All 30 teams sim every week, standings and draft values update
  in real time
- Legacy system: Hall of Fame, franchise records, and legacy score persist
  across all seasons

1.4 Platform
------------
Platform:   Web browser (desktop first)
Framework:  React + Vite
Styling:    Tailwind CSS with custom vintage palette
Database:   Supabase (PostgreSQL)
Hosting:    TBD

2. CORE GAME LOOP
================================================================================

2.1 Season Structure
--------------------
A season consists of four phases. The weekly tick is the heartbeat — every
week all 30 teams advance simultaneously.

  Early (Weeks 1-6):   Persona reveals, dev ticks begin, all-star voting opens
  Mid (Weeks 7-12):    Trade deadline, all-star break, playoff race begins
  Late (Weeks 13-24):  Playoff push, awards race, chemistry peaks
  Playoffs:            3 rounds, best of 3 series, top 6 teams
  Offseason:           Extensions, draft, free agency, pathway resets, goal

2.2 Weekly Tick Sequence
------------------------
Every week the following happens in order, simultaneously for all 30 teams:

  1. Your game simulates with full play-by-play and key moments
  2. All 29 AI games simulate mathematically in the background
  3. Standings recalculate for all 30 teams
  4. Development ticks fire for every player on every roster
  5. Chemistry pairs update for your roster
  6. Story beats check triggers for your team
  7. Draft pick projected values update based on new standings
  8. Dashboard refreshes with new world state

2.3 New Game Setup
------------------
4-step onboarding flow styled as a vintage front office appointment:

  Step 1: Enter manager name — persists on every screen as GM: [Name]
  Step 2: Choose league — fictional NBA-inspired structure, 30 teams
  Step 3: Choose team — cards with rating (A-D), difficulty tag, flavor text
  Step 4: Confirm appointment — styled as official memo, Accept the Position

3.2 Player Persona System
--------------------------
8 primary categories, 7-10 sub-personas each. Hidden at generation, reveals
after half a season of observation.

  Raw Diamond         Young, unpolished, high ceiling. 10 sub-personas.
  Quiet Assassin      Low maintenance, clutch performer. 10 sub-personas.
  Locker Room Cancer  Elite talent that corrodes chemistry. 10 sub-personas.
  Underdog            Hidden will, outperforms rating. 10 sub-personas.
  Fading Legend       Veteran in decline, still gives. 10 sub-personas.
  Franchise Cornerstone  Born to lead, demands commitment. 10 sub-personas.
  Mercenary           Purely transactional, no loyalty. 9 sub-personas.
  Late Bloomer        Looks average, surprise arc incoming. 10 sub-personas.

3.3 Development Pathway System
--------------------------------
8 pathways per player per season. Persona-pathway compatibility creates
synergy (up to 1.5x growth) or conflict (down to 0.55x with morale hit).
Mid-season switch incurs a growth pause penalty.

  Slasher       Finishing, speed, athleticism. Tags: Relentless, Foul Magnet
  Sharpshooter  Shooting, off-ball movement. Tags: Hot Hand, Corner Specialist
  Floor General Playmaking, IQ. Tags: Dimer, Court Vision
  Lockdown      Defense, any position. Tags: Pest, Stopper
  Stretch Big   Shooting for bigs. Tags: Floor Spacer, Face-Up Threat
  Enforcer      Interior, rebounding. Tags: Brick Wall, Rim Protector
  Facilitator   Passing, hustle. Tags: Glue Guy, Secondary Playmaker
  Two-Way       Balanced growth. Tags: Swiss Army, Two-Way Player

3.4 Game Simulation Engine
---------------------------
How winning and losing works:

  Team Strength = (Starter avg overall x chemistry mult) + coach bonus + home court

  Chemistry multipliers:
    70+ chemistry = 1.03x boost
    40-69         = 1.0x neutral
    Below 40      = 0.97x penalty

  Home court = flat +3 to home team strength
  Coach bonus = (best coach level / 5) x 2 — max +2

  Variance = +-8 applied independently to each team
  Final scores map to realistic range: 85-115 points

  5 key moment types:
    Momentum swing   One team on a run, banner fires in feed
    Clutch time      Final 2 minutes of close games
    Persona moment   Player-specific card with flavor from persona sub
    Injury scare     Player exits feed, status update pending
    Breakout alert   Player having an outlier performance

  GM interventions:
    Substitution    Fresh player gets 1.05x multiplier on next 3 possessions
    Timeout         Resets opponent run counter during momentum swing
    Play call       Routes possession through highest-overall player (1.08x)
    Double team     Reduces opponent clutch probability by 40%

3.5 Team Chemistry
-------------------
Pair scores (0-100) roll up to team score, weighted by minutes played.
4 drivers update scores weekly:
  - Persona compatibility ceiling (determines max possible score)
  - Time together (+1 per week, capped at ceiling)
  - Big moments (wins +5, losses -4, trade demands -5 to all)
  - Coach personality (dev specialist = passive +5 per season)

Relationship types:
  90-100  Best friends  +dev speed 15%, sim boost
  70-89   Bonded        +dev speed 10%, small sim boost
  50-69   Neutral       No effect
  30-49   Tense         Slight sim penalty
  0-29    Toxic/Beef    Morale hit, story beat fires, possible trade demand

6. LEGACY & LONG-TERM PROGRESSION
================================================================================

6.1 Legacy Score Points
------------------------
  Championship win              +500
  Playoff appearance            +100
  Award winner on roster        +75
  Hall of Fame induction        +150
  Franchise record broken       +25
  Franchise goal achieved       +200
  Player developed 60 to 80+    +100
  Persona evolved positively    +30
  Missing franchise goal        -100
  Star forced out via demand    -50
  Locker room incident unresolved  -25
  Finished last in league       -75
  (Score floors at 0 — never goes negative)

6.2 Hall of Fame
-----------------
Players need to meet at least 3 of 8 criteria:
  - 4+ seasons played
  - 85+ overall at peak
  - 1+ championship
  - 1+ individual award
  - 85+ chemistry bond with teammate
  - Signature move unlocked
  - Persona evolved positively
  - 18+ avg points per season

Induction ceremony: player name, career stats, quirk displayed final time,
one-sentence legacy line generated from career data.

6.3 Franchise Goal Types
-------------------------
  make_playoffs, win_championship, develop_player, reach_record,
  rebuild, prove_yourself, dynasty

  Hit the goal: +200 legacy. Miss it: -100 legacy. Next season's options
  reflect the outcome.
